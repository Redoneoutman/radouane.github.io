July-August 2022

Technical Internship: Design and Development of a connected device to help diagnose heart disease by ECG

FDRISI - UM6SS - Casablanca

In my recent project, I undertook the challenging task of designing and developing a connected device aimed at assisting in the diagnosis of heart disease through the use of electrocardiography (ECG). Leveraging my knowledge of embedded systems, I opted to employ an ESP device as the core component of the system. By integrating this versatile microcontroller into the device, I was able to establish seamless communication with other devices and systems, thanks to its built-in Wi-Fi capabilities. This connectivity proved to be crucial in transmitting real-time ECG data to healthcare professionals, enabling remote monitoring and analysis of patients' cardiac health. Through careful design and development, my project successfully combined the power of an embedded system with the convenience of wireless communication to create an innovative tool for the early detection and diagnosis of heart disease, ultimately contributing to improved patient care and well-being.
